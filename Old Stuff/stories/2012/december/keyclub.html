Key Club

Are you interested in building leadership skills and doing school and community service? Then join the Key Club! This is the first year that Harborfields High School has a Key Club. To make this club a success, it needs people to join. Anyone is welcome to come. At the first meeting there was an election for the club officers. As of now they still need a junior and senior class representative. There was also a discussion about community service opportunities.
