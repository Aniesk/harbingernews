About NHS

Advisors: Mr. Khan and Ms Harmon

Room: Auditorium

The Gloria Vavasseur chapter of National Honor Society is composed of a select group of students who demonstrate excellence in scholarship, leadership, service, and character. Students are eligible to apply for membership at the end of their sophomore year. Applicants must have a 90 weighted average, and they must exhibit active participation in their school and their community. NHS members are required to complete 40 hours of community service and 5 hours of peer tutoring each year to maintain their membership. Some of the activities that the National Honor Society participates in include: HHS Open School Night, the Breast Cancer Walk at Jones Beach, the Light the Night Walk for Leukemia and Lymphoma, Safe Halloween, the PTSA Barnes & Noble fundraiser, the Huntington Adult Day Care Sweetheart Ball, the HACEF Carnival, Relay for Life, the Washington Drive 2nd Grade Carnival, and they currently run the cans and bottles recycling program at the high school.


This club meets on alternating Tuesdays at 2:30. See advisors for more details.
